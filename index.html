About Livesley Plumbing

Based in Heathcote, Christchurch, Livesley Plumbing Limited proudly services the Canterbury region with professional, reliable plumbing services.

We specialize in all residential plumbing work, semi-commercial plumbing work, plumbing maintenance and emergency plumbing services. From leaking taps to hot water cylinder repairs and more, we've got you covered.

Our motto is simple: "For everything plumbing, we can do it. No job too big or small." We're tried and trusted by our community, delivering quality workmanship with honest pricing.
